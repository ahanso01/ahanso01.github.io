Addison Hanson

Addison Hanson was born on April 21, 1996 in Philadelphia, PA along with his twin brother Watson. He was born and raised in Radnor, PA with his two brothers, parents and his dog Nellie. He attended Radnor High School where he wrestled and played ultimate frisbee. He now is a junior at Tufts University where he is a Computer Science major and an ELS minor. He plays ultimate frisbee here at Tufts with the Elephant Men. He is also a brother of the Theta Delta Chi Fraternity here on campus as well.
